Tour Details

  • Pick from Lahore and travel to Shogran, short time stay at Kiwai waterfall, shift into jeep, move to Shogran, check in hotel and overnight stay in Shogran.
  • After breakfast visit Siri, Paye Lake, use jeep track for back to hotel and move towards Naran, check in hotel, Batakundi and overnight stay in Batakundi.
  • After breakfast visit Jhalkat , Lolosar Lake, short time stay on Babusar Top and move to Hunza, check in hotel and night stay in Hunza.
  • After breakfast visit Altit Fort, Baltit Fort, Eagle’s Nest and night stay in Karimabad.
  • After breakfast visit Attabad Lake, Hussaini Hanging Bridge, Passu Cone, Khunjerab National Park, short time stay at China Border, back to hotel and night stay in Hunza.
  • After breakfast travel to Nomal - KKH Rd shift into jeep, move to Naltar Valley, visit Naltar Lake, Pari Lake, Satrangi Lake, check in hotel and night stay in Minapin Hotel.
  • After breakfast visit Rakaposhi View, 3 world highest Mountains Junction, Babusar Top and move towards Naran check in hotel and night stay in Naran.
  • After breakfast visit Saif-ul-Maluk Lake (jeep track), Rafting in Kunhar River, back to hotel, take a rest, visit Naran bazar and night stay.
  • After breakfast travel to Islamabad, visit Muzaffarabad View, Kashmir waterfall, Neelum River then move towards Islamabad, check in hotel, take a rest, visit Monal, back to hotel and night stay in Islamabad.
  • After breakfast visit Taxila Museum, Khan Pur Dam, Faisal Masjid, Centaurus Mall and then move back to Lahore for end of tour.
