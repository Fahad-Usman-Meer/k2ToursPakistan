Tour Details

  • Pick from Lahore and travel to Islamabad via motorway and move towards Muzaffarabad, short time stay at Neelum point, visit Kashmir Waterfall, Muzaffarabad view, check in Muzaffarabad hotel and overnight stay in Muzaffarabad.
  • After breakfast, travel to Sharda, visit Dhani Waterfall, move to Keran, visit Ath-Muqam, Kundal Shahi Waterfall, move towards Sharda, check in hotel and overnight stay in Sharda.
  • After breakfast visit Sharda, move towards Kel, shift into cable car, visit Arang Kel, back to Sharda and overnight Stay in Sharda.
  • After breakfast, move to Ratti Gali Lake (jeep track) and move towards Keran, visit Neelum & Upper Neelum, check in hotel and overnight stay in Keran.
  • After breakfast, travel to Muzaffarabad, visit Peer Chanasi, check in hotel and overnight stay in Muzaffarabad.
  • After breakfast, travel to Lahore, visit Domal River, Jannat Waterfall and then move back to Lahore for end of tour.
