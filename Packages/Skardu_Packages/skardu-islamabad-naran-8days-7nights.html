Tour Details

  • Pick from Islamabad and travel to Naran, short time stay at Kiwai Waterfall, move towards Naran, check in hotel and overnight stay in Naran.
  • After breakfast visit Jhalkat, Lolosar Lake, short time stay at Babusar Top and move towards Astore, check in hotel and night stay in Astore.
  • After breakfast travel to Rama Valley, visit Rama Lake (jeep + hacking track), back to Astore and night stay in Astore.
  • Early morning after breakfast on the way travel to Deosai, Sheosar Lake, Bara Pani, Satpara Lake, reach Skardu, check in hotel and overnight stay in Skardu.
  • After breakfast visit Shangrila, Upper Kachura Lake and Lower Kachura Lake, back to hotel and night stay in Skardu.
  • After breakfast visit Cold Desert, Shigar Fort and Khaplu Fort, back to hotel and night stay in Skardu.
  • After Breakfast move towards Chilas via Skardu Karakoram Road and Check Into hotel and night stay in Chilas.
  • Early morning after breakfast travel to Islamabad via Babusar Top, short time stay at Kashmir Waterfall, visit Neelum River and then move back to Islamabad for end of tour.
