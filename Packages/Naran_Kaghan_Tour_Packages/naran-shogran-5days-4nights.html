Tour Details

  • Pick from lahore & travel to shogran, visit neelum river, Kashmir waterfall, short time stay kiwai waterfall, take a jeep move to shogran check in hotel & overnight stay in shogran.
  • After breakfast visit siri lake, paye, jeep track back to hotel & move towards naran, check in hotel batakundi, overnight stay in batakundi.
  • After breakfast visit Jalkhat, Lolosar lake, short time stay on Babusar top & travel back to Naran check in hotel and night stay.
  • After breakfast travel to saif ul maluk lake, jeep track 2 or 3 hours stay, visit lake, Rafting in kunhar river, visit naran bazar and overnight stay in naran.
  • Breakfast and then move back to Lahore.
