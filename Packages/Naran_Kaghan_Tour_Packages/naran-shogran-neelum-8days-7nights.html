Tour Details

  • Pick from Lahore and travel to naran, Short Time stay balakot kunhar river view, kiwai waterfall, move towards naran, check in hotel & overnight stay in naran.
  • After breakfast, travel to Saif ul maluk lake, jeep track 2 & 3 hours, stay back to naran, rafting in kunhar river and overnight stay in hotel.
  • After breakfast, visit lulusar lake, move to babusar top, Short Time stay on babusar top and move towards shogran, take a jeep, check in hotel and overnight stay in shogran.
  • After breakfast, visiting Siri lake, paye jeep track, back to hotel check out hotel and move towards muzaffarabad, visit muzaffarabad view, check in hotel and overnight stay in muzaffarabad.
  • After breakfast, move to neelum valley, visit kutton waterfall, 8 mokam, upper neelum view, check in hotel upper neelum and overnight stay.
  • After breakfast, visit sharda, take a jeep visit Kel, arang Kel back to Keran check in hotel overnight stay in Keran.
  • After breakfast, visit ratti gali lake, jeep track and move to muzaffarabad, visit peer chanasi and overnight stay in muzaffarabad.
  • After breakfast, visit domal river, Kashmir waterfall, short time stay neelum river and then move back to Lahore.
